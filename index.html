About Us

Rooted in Bangladesh.
Reaching the World.

Naim Agro Export Ltd is a Dhaka-based agro export company with deep roots in Bangladesh's fertile farmlands. We source directly from trusted farmers across the country, ensuring every product meets international export standards before it leaves our hands.

From the rich soil of Bangladesh's agricultural heartland, we bring potatoes, seasonal vegetables, and tropical fruits to buyers across Asia, the Middle East, and beyond — with consistency, transparency, and competitive pricing.

🌱
Farm-to-Port

Direct sourcing from growers eliminates intermediaries and ensures freshness.

📋
Compliant Documentation

All exports comply with phytosanitary, customs, and destination-country regulations.

🤝
Buyer-First Partnership

We build long-term relationships with buyers through reliability and honest trade.
